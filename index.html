Currently

I am attending UC Davis in prusuit of a BA in Design and a minor in Art History. I am facinated by the interaction between human and tool, and am especially interested in narrowing the gap between the tool-maker and the tool-user. In less academic terms, I want to make life more humane for both product teams and end users.

Outside of design, I enjoy time outdoors, latin dance, and working with my hands. I am a senior volunteer (also know as a "minister") at the Davis Bike Collective. I am saving up for my very own DSLR, and a new pair of ballroom shoes. ;)

In The Future

I want to continute working at the junction of people and the objects and programs we create. I am seeking positions in UX/UI Design, UX Research, Front-End Development, and combinations of the three. I love the infinite possibilities of digital prodcuts, but if the opportunity to work with physical industrial presents itself, I would be more than thrilled.

Keep in touch to hear more about my bike mechanics + arduino passion project. I'll post about it eventually!
